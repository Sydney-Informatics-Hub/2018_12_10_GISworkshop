The Sydney Informatics Hub is a Core Research Facility of the University of Sydney. SIH provides support, training, and expertise on research data, analyses and computing for research staff, students and affiliates of the University. We deliver policies, systems, advice, engineering and training to research staff, students and affiliates of the University. This workshop is fully developed by SIH and will cover FIXME. Participants will be encouraged to help one another and to apply what they have learned to their own research problems.
